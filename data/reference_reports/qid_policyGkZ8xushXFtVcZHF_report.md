## Why Primary Sources Are Essential for This Brief
- The brief requires precise elements for each case: official penalty amount, date, enforcement type (settlement vs. CMP), detailed breach description and affected individuals, exact Security Rule CFR citations (e.g., 45 CFR §164.308(a)(1)(ii)(A) for risk analysis), OCR’s findings/quotes, and the full Corrective Action Plan obligations.
- These elements are only reliably obtainable from the HHS/OCR enforcement web pages and the linked resolution agreements/CAPs or CMP notices. Secondary reporting cannot meet the “primary sources only” and “immediate URL next to every fact” requirements.
