## Notes on Policy Design If Considering Change
- Age choice: A 19 threshold (not 18) is the only option that plausibly avoids direct high-school spillovers, but evidence still predicts higher harms than MLDA 21. Strong, well-funded safeguards would be necessary (e.g., universal <21 zero-tolerance, high-visibility DUI enforcement, frequent compliance checks including delivery, server training, social-host and furnishing enforcement). Federal reform would be needed to avoid highway-fund penalties. Evidence indicates the safest course remains MLDA 21; any pilot should include rigorous independent evaluation and sunset provisions.
